workbeer

A craft beer workplace experience.

How it Works

Does your team have a bad case of the Monday’s…but like, everyday? Workbeer was created to bring happy hour to you. Skip the awkward “let’s all grab a beer after work” scene where Janet in accounting decides to drink through her issues and Chris from HR is working through a rough patch and say yes to having a couple of beers in a controlled, fun, and epicurean environment.

Workbeer brings a couple of craft beer drinking pros to you, brews in hand. We’ll let you try some great local beers you can find on tap around town, and some you can only find in certain areas of the state. Looking at all of the taps in a bar can be overwhelming at times, but Workbeer offers a friendly approach to the craft beer scene so you can say, “Move over Mr. Hipster, craft beer is anyone’s game!” The best part about craft beer drinkers is their promiscuity; that’s why we offer beers from almost every brewery in the state!



Say yes to team building, say yes to local, but most importantly…say yes to craft beer.

Booking a Reservation

To request a reservation, click the button below to fill out the request form. Please be as accurate as possible when selecting your party size — we have to be sure we bring enough beer! Reservations are limited, so we’ll be fulfilling requests on a first-come, first-serve basis.

If we can fulfill your reservation, we’ll be in touch by email to work out the details.

Workbeer is a flat-rate $250 for a one-hour reservation for a party up to 20 people. Larger parties can be accommodated for a separate fee.
